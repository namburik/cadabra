Scaling ML inference in production

Practical patterns to serve models reliably at scale.

Overview

Avoid black-box assumptions: measure tail latencies, set clear SLOs, and prefer simple orchestration patterns that your ops team can operate.

Key takeaways

  • Start with synchronous microservices for low volume, then add async workers for batch work.
  • Monitor model quality drift and automate rollback paths.
  • Optimize cost using batching, quantization, and right-sized instances.
