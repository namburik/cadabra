paytek: Live Payments Agent

May 2026 Hands-on By Kishore Namburi

Paytek is a conversational AI platform that automates merchant relationships with payment processors.

Instead of filling out long forms, a merchant just chats — the AI extracts their business details, validates them in real time, and submits the request through a multi-agent workflow that routes approvals, flags risk, and tracks state across platforms like Stripe, PayPal, and Adyen.

What makes it different: it's not a chatbot wrapper. It's a full agentic system — specialized agents for boarding, servicing, hierarchy management, and workflow orchestration — all running through a single conversational interface backed by Gemini, AWS, Neo4J, and OpenID auth.
